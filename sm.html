Description

Leader of Tomorrow started out as a MUNing club. So Summit Manipal is essentially us returning to our roots. While we send delegations to collegiate level Model United Nations across the country, in the even semester we host up to 300 students for our flagship event, Summit Manipal, one of the oldest MUNs in the country. Since our inception we have always tried to broaden the spectrum of debates, and with that goal in mind Summit Manipal is designed to cater to first timers as well as experienced debaters.

Our committees vary in their level of difficulty, and we pride ourselves in selecting and Executive Board that guide the committees to ensure and enriching experience for everyone. The Summit Manipal motto- ‘Ductus Exemplo’ is a reflection of our endeavor. We don’t want to just witness great debate, we’d like to be the ones to create it. This year’s edition has four committees to choose from, with agendas that cater to the issues we read and hear about on a daily basis. MUNing is one of oldest forms of civilized debate, and we take that seriously.

Summit Manipal is open to students across the country, and internationally. We organize our eleventh edition with the hopes to create a space where individuals can experience well rounded debate of the highest order.

UNGA DISEC

The General assembly of the United Nations is one of the six main organs of the UN and the only one in which all the member states have equal representation. One nation has only one vote. all 193 member states of the UN are represented in this unique forum to discuss and work together on a wide array of issues covered by the UN charter.

ECOSOC

Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC), one of the six principal organs of the United Nations (UN). It is responsible for the direction and coordination of the economic, social, humanitarian, and cultural activities carried out by the UN. ECOSOC serves as the central forum for discussing international economic and social issues.

Bilderberg

The Bilderberg Conference is not a body of the United Nations, but rather a private conference held annually where some of the most influential people in the world meet on an invite based only system and discuss agendas that are left undisclosed to the public.Since its inaugural meeting in 1954, Bilderberg has been an annual forum for informal discussions.

UNSC

The Security Council is the most powerful organ within the UN with the sole authority to use force in response to threats to international peace and security. The committee fails to disappoint with an extremely high level of debate and truly substantive solutions which try to solve the world’s most violent conflicts.

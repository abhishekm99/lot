Description

In keeping with the name of our club, we also host an event exclusively for the first years at Manipal Institute of Technology. Once the interaction ban is lifted, one of the first events for the freshers is Cambiar. Driven by publicity and public relations, Cambiar provides a platform for 20 of the college's clubs to showcase themselves, and help the incoming batch find their place in collegiate life.

An event that unifies clubs from both technical and cultural sides, Cambiar is essentially the coming together of our multi-faceted college, to provide a home away from home for all students

Description

Leaders of Tomorrow’s Youth Parliament is a simulation of the Indian Parliament. Though the event aims to be a simulation of the different houses of the parliament and follows parliamentary methodology, rules of procedure are altered every year to keep a fresh format and make it more challenging as well as interesting for participants. We often also attempt to simulate different organizations of the Indian government to offer participants more variety.

Participants focus on discussing relevant issues of national importance. Our debates are formatted to ensure quality discourse, and the participants are judged by able and experienced Executive Boards. The Youth Parliament, is open to all students and attracts participants from all colleges under Manipal University as well as outstation participants. With different committees for different levels of debate, it acts as an opportunity for first timers to learn parliamentary procedure and for seasoned debaters to practice.

The Youth Parliament is an opportunity to meet like-minded individuals and debate issues relevant to us all, in today’s times.

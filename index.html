About Leaders Of Tomorrow

Leaders of Tomorrow is the primary MIT club that organizes Youth Parliaments and the national level Model United Nations, Summit Manipal. As reflected by our Summit motto, “Ductus Exemplo” we take pride in following ethics, thereby leading by example in our aim to cultivate an atmosphere of rigorous and well balanced debates on topics of national and international importance through such events.

Our Events

Summit Manipal


Leader of Tomorrow started out as a MUNing club. So Summit Manipal is essentially us returning to our roots. While we send delegations to collegiate level Model United Nations across the country, in the even semester we host up to 300 students for our flagship event, Summit Manipal, one of the oldest MUNs in the country.

Learn More

Youth Parliament


Leaders of Tomorrow’s Youth Parliament is a simulation of the Indian Parliament. Though the event aims to be a simulation of the different houses of the parliament and follows parliamentary methodology, rules of procedure are altered every year to keep a fresh format and make it more challenging as well as interesting for participants.

Cambiar


In keeping with the name of our club, we also host an event exclusively for the first years at Manipal Institute of Technology. Once the interaction ban is lifted, one of the first events for the freshers is Cambiar.
